Chili

Ingredients

  • 16oz ground beef (or turkey)
  • 7oz can salsa verde
  • 15oz can black beans
  • 1 can tomato soup
  • Chili powder
  • 2 or 3 roma tomatoes
  • 1 medium diced onion (for topping)
  • Shredded cheddar cheese (for topping)

Preparation

  1. Cook the ground meat in a medium pan until fully cooked. Drain the fat and place in a small crock pot. I usually do this step the night before.
  2. Pour in salsa verde, black beans, and tomato soup. Add chili powder until the spicyness level you want.
  3. Cook for 8 hours on low.
  4. After 4 or 5 hours add in chopped roma tomatoes.
  5. Serve diced onion and shredded cheddar cheese as toppings.
