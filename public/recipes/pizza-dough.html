Pizza Dough

Ingredients

  • 8 cups All-Purpose Flour or Polselli Classica
  • 2 tablespoons yeast
  • Pinch of sugar
  • Olive oil
  • Salt

Preparation

  1. Add the flour to a large bowl.
  2. In a small bowl add the yeast and a pinch of sugar.
  3. Add about 1/2 cup of warm water to the yeast blend.
  4. Add a generous pour of olive oil to the flour mix.
  5. Add the yeast blend to the flour. Add salt to taste.
  6. Stir until well combined.
  7. Add a generous pour of olive oil to your pizza stone / pan.
  8. Pour the flour mixture into the middle of the pan. Flour your hands and spread the mixture across the pan.
  9. Cover with a dishcloth and allow to sit for 2 hours to rise.
  10. Spread your pizza sauce and bake!
